About Jyoti Girls PG

Jyoti Girls PG has supported aspiring engineers, doctors, chartered accountants, and young professionals in Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar for over a decade. We are a women-led team committed to delivering the warmth of a family home with the discipline of a premium hostel. Our building is purpose-designed for resident comfort, featuring cross-ventilated rooms, multiple study areas, and community lounges that encourage collaborative learning.

The neighbourhood around Osmanpura offers the perfect balance of convenience and calm. Daily groceries, pharmacies, and medical assistance are available within a one-kilometre radius, while the buzzing Deogiri college and Osmanpura coaching corridors are accessible on foot. Parents appreciate the well-lit streets, proximity to public transport.

We are also environmentally conscious. Solar panels power the common areas, waste is segregated at source, and a rainwater harvesting system supports the landscaped courtyard. Parents can stay updated through our monthly digital newsletter that shares mess menus, events, and maintenance updates in real time.

Frequently Asked Questions

What room types and tariffs are available?

We offer double and triple sharing rooms and four sharing . Double sharing starts at INR 4,800 per month, while triple sharing starts at INR 4,300 per month and four sharing starts at 3,800. Plans are excluded of meals.

Is the mess food included in the monthly rent?

No. We have a seperate cafe near by named "Cafe Aveen's". Along with home delivered tiffins we also serve various food items such as Maggi, Omelette, Biryani and other.

How secure is the property?

The building is a private gated property with CCTV coverage in common areas, 24x7 female staff, and strict visitor policies. Parents are informed instantly about any late-night movement.

How do I reserve a bed?

Submit the enquiry form or contact us on call or WhatsApp. We will confirm the avaliablility, followed by a token advance to confirm the booking.

Can parents visit their ward?

Parents are welcome during visiting hours every weekend.
